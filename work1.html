How to get my clothing design made

Your idea

blueprint, design and Tech Pack

01

It all start with you providing us with a blueprint of your clothing idea. It details out everything related from fabric & design construction to packaging of finished garments

Prototype

Pattern grading and sample making

02

After finalisation of tech packs, we make a prototype of your clothing design which confirms mainly the fitting, stitching quality and design.

Finalize

Approvals for bulk production

03

Bulk production is the final step inside our clothing factory. The lead time usually is around 30 - 45 days depending upon the design complexity of the order.

Dispatch

Dispatch, Logistics & Reorder

04

Your clothing products are exported in a water resistant sealed packaging. We also arrange door-to-door delivery via air or sea.
